VROX Network Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 18, 2026

At VROX Network, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1

Information Collection

To provide a personalized experience and maintain network integrity, we collect the following:

  • Full Name: Used for account identification within the app.
  • Country: Collected for regional analytics and community distribution.
  • Profile Picture: The app allows you to upload a profile picture from your gallery. We use Cloudinary (a third-party cloud service) to securely store and host your photo.

2

Age Restriction (18+ Only)

VROX Network is strictly for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a user is under the required age, we reserve the right to terminate the account and delete all associated data immediately.

3

Data Storage and Deletion

Security: Your personal data (Name and Country) is stored securely using Supabase, while your profile image is managed via Cloudinary.

Account Deletion: We provide a clear option for account deletion within the app. Once deleted, all your personal records (Name, Country, Profile Photo, and Mined Balance) are permanently removed from both Supabase and Cloudinary.

4

Advertising and Third-Party Services

To keep our services free, we use Google AdMob:

  • Ad IDs: We use Android Advertising IDs to show relevant ads.
  • Device Info: AdMob may collect your device's unique ID and IP address to optimize ad delivery and prevent fraud.

5

Security

VROX Network uses industry-standard protocols to protect your data. As we build this network, your security and data integrity remain our top priorities.

6

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

7

Contact Us

For any questions regarding this policy or your data, please contact us at: support@vroxnetwork.com
