Frequently Asked Questions

How to start mining?
Go to the Mine screen and tap 'START MINING'. Your session will last for 24 hours, after which you need to restart it.
What are Quests?
Quests are simple tasks like following our social media or watching ads. Completing them earns you instant VROX rewards! Check the Quests section on the Home screen.
How does the Lucky Wheel work?
You can spin the Lucky Wheel to win various VROX bonuses. Each spin requires a small cooldown period, so keep checking back to try your luck!
What are Daily Rewards?
Daily Rewards allow you to claim free VROX every day just by logging in. The rewards increase if you maintain a daily streak!
How does the referral system work?
You get a permanent mining rate boost of +0.25 VROX/hr for every friend who joins using your unique referral code.
What is the mining boost feature?
On the Mine screen, you can watch a short video to boost your mining rate by 10% for the next 1 hour.
When can I withdraw my balance?
Withdrawal functionality will be enabled as we achieve key milestones in our Roadmap. We are working towards a secure and seamless process for all users—stay tuned for official announcements! 🚀
Is Vrox Network mining free?
Yes, cloud mining on Vrox Network is completely free and doesn't consume your phone's hardware resources or battery.
How is the token price calculated?
The price is dynamic and increases based on the total coins mined globally. As the community grows, the value of VROX increases.

If you couldn't find the answer to your question in our FAQs, feel free to reach out to us. We're here to help!
